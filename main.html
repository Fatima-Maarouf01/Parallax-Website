Welcome

In this presentation we'll be explaining AI and chat GPT. We'll also mention how AI evolved and what will become of it in the future, as well as the impact that AI will have in various fields such as education, transportation and healthcare

What is AI

Artificial Intelligence is simply granting machines the ability to simulate human Intelligance and allowing these machines to perform tasks that no ordinary computer can do, tasks that require a high level of intelligance and are only achieved via learning

How does AI work

By Implementing machine learning algorithms on neural networks (structured similar to our brains) we can allow the computer to learn new information, and acheive new results all on its own. Then the computer will be able to decide which actions are favorable and which are not. You can see a type of a neural network to the right!

Examples of AI

Multiple AI projects are available. From self driving cars to online chatbots and actual robots like Sophia. We also have AI than can fully generate Music! However the innovation of the decade is chat GPT, a language model that you can chat with. It can do anything from solving equations to writing code, and even writing poetry!

Is AI a Threat

The vast improvements that AI is witnessing is causing a considerable amount of panic among people. Questions about AI replacing humans in many work fields, leaving millions unemployed are being asked. The impact on the market is inevitable, some people will loose their jobs to AI, but on the other hand this change in the market will provide a ton of new job opportunities!
